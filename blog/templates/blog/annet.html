I am a test engineer, philosopher and animal-lover with a passion for acquiring knowledge and learning new skills. I am often engaged in studying several topics at the same time.

Already at a very young age I was fascinated with computers. As a little girl I ‘tested’ all options of our MS-DOS machine. At the age of twelve I learned making websites, using HTML, and enquired into the basics of C++. Last year, taking the MOOC ‘introduction into Java Programming’ sparked my interest in studying computer science. Next to writing my philosophy MA thesis, I took courses in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, SQL and PHP. With this knowledge I developed the wordpress theme for www.driesterren.com and helped creating www.diogenesclub.nl.

It is my ambition to keep on enhancing my knowledge about testing and developing software.

At the moment I am studying for the Java 6 certification and I am learning to code in JavaScript via teamtreehouse.com.

At Sogeti, I started the community ‘Testing with Java’ to share my knowledge and enthusiasm with colleques.
